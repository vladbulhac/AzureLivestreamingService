Settings

  • Step 1: Go to Settings panel on the lower-right side

  • Step 2: In the beginning, configure your settings using the example below

    Encoder: NVIDIA NVENC or any x264 for AMD graphics cards

    Rate Control: CBR

    Bitrate: 2500 Kbps

    Keyframe interval: 2 seconds

    Preset: Performance

    Profile: high

    GPU: 0

    Max B-frames: 2

    Experiment with the settings until you find the best ones for your computer.

  • Step 3: Input in the Server field the ingest URL

    Ingest URL: {{ingestUrl}}

    Stream Key can be any value.

 Start livestream

  • Step 1: Click on the Start Streaming button

    To end your livestream click on the Stop Streaming button.
